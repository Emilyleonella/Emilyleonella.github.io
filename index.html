Welcome to Ecuador

Home to the Galapagos Islands

Ecuador is a beautful country on South America's west coast and located on the equator. It borders Colombia to the North, and Peru to the South. It is home to some of the tallest mountains in South America, and to beautfuil wildlife in both the Galapagos Islands and the Amazon rainforest. The capital of our beautiful country, is Quito, which is located in the mountains. You can find amazing local food, exciting activities, and local markets with traditional Ecuadorian clothing all throughout Quito.
